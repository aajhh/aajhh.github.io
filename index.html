aajhh

is a multidisciplinary artist from the north of England.

His work explores themes of mental health, phobia, strangeness, and the horror of having a mind and body that seem not under your full control.

He expanded on these themes in his BA and MA, producing a dissertation titled Illustrating the Incomprehensible: How artists visualise that which was designed to be unvisualisable and a body of work titled THE HOUSE I GREW OLD IN.

He is also a character designer and character illustrator, enjoying pulling aesthetics and designs from historical clothing and religious history.

⚫︎⚫︎⚫︎⚫︎⚫︎

In his spare time, aajhh also creates custom content for the Sims 4, which he releases for free, as well as being a solo video game dev. He is currently working on a point and click game (title TBD) which he hopes to release in 2025.
