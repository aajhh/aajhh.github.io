DESCRIPTION

Untitled Whaling Game is an upcoming cooking game by aajhh. It is a cosmic horror cooking game, taking place on a 19th century whaling ship, in which you must manage the morale of your crew by cooking satisfying meals.





HISTORY

Untitled Whaling Game is aajhh's first non-rpgmaker non-text based game. He had the idea for a "cooking mama with a sanity meter" style game for a few years, however he was not sure he was skilled enough to create the game to a level of polish he could be proud of. After taking a small JS course and researching games that were also created/used elements of JS in their code, he finally felt like he had a solid enough knowledge base to dive in and work on a full game. As he developed the game it evolved from a vague idea to a mechanically satisfying experience that incorporates resource management, cooking minigames, and hard and long lasting decision making.





FEATURES

  • four cooking minigames (3/4 implemented - Feb 2024)
  • flexible recipes that respond to your available and used ingredients
  • 2-bit colour palettes, with 6 to choose from
  • hand drawn assets, including low-poly style 3D models where needed
  • characters who you will come to love and miss
  • contains explicit cannibalism and animal death
